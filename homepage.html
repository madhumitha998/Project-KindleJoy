SOLUTIONS

We came up with solutions to improve the Kindle Garden's learning experience to become more inclusive, experiential and safe.

kindle BENCH
PROBLEM

THE CHILDREN, ESPECIALLY THOSE WITH SPECIAL NEEDS, MIGHT HAVE DIFFICULTY IN COMMUNICATING WITH OTHERS.

SOLUTION

SINCE CHILDREN ARE ATTRACTED TO MUSIC, WE CREATED A MUSICAL BENCH FOR CHILDREN TO SIT AT AND PLAY TOGETHER WITH. ON TOP OF BEING A MUSICAL BENCH, IT ALSO ACTS AS A BUDDY BENCH FOR LONELY KIDS TO SIT AT. TEACHERS CAN THEN TRY TO ENGAGE THE KIDS WHEN THEY ARE SITTING AT THE BENCH.

LIMITATION

AS WE HAVE NO SPEAKER (AND THE BUZZER IS WEAK), OUR PROTOTYPE RELIES ON LED LIGHTS TO MIMIC THE MUSICAL ASPECT.

IMPROVEMENT

WE COULD INCLUDE OCCASION-THEMED SONGS (SUCH AS CHRISTMAS SONGS), USE BETTER SPEAKER TO PLAY RANDOMISED SONGS AND ADD MORE COLOURFUL LIGHTS TO INCREASE THE ATTRACTIVENESS OF THE BENCH.

DATA COULD BE COLLECTED TO ANALYSE HOW MANY PEOPLE SAT ON THE BENCH AND HOW LONG WAS IT USED FOR.

kindle ZOO
PROBLEM

TO INCREASE THE INCLUSIVITY OF SPECIAL NEEDS CHILDREN BY GIVING THEM MORE OPPORTUNITIES TO UNDERSTAND AND COMMUNICATE WITH ONE ANOTHER IN THE CLASSROOMS

SOLUTION

IT IS A SIMPLE GAME WHERE A SPECIAL NEEDS STUDENT WILL PAIR UP WITH AN ABLE-BODIED STUDENT TO WORK TOGETHER AND FIND ANIMALS. THIS ALLOWS FOR MORE COMMUNICATION AND LEARNING IN AN INCLUSIVE MANNER

KINDLE ZOO WEBSITE
LIMITATION

THE SCHOOL WOULD HAVE TO INVEST IN MORE IPADS.

IMPROVEMENT

WE COULD INCLUDE MORE ELEMENTS IN THE GAMES SUCH AS CHALLENGES THAT THE PAIR CAN COMPLETE WHEN THEY FIND AN ANIMAL.

kindle SAFE
PROBLEM

DURING ACTIVITIES OR EXCURSIONS, FACILITATORS MAY BE BUSY WITH OTHER CHILDREN AND MIGHT NOT NOTICE IMMEDIATELY IF A CHILD STRAYS FROM THE GROUP. THEY ALSO HAVE NO WAY TO QUICKLY LOCATE THE CHILD.

SOLUTION

TO MAKE IT EASIER TO FIND OUT WHO IS MISSING AND HOW LONG THE CHILD HAS GONE MISSING FOR.

KINDLE SAFE WEBSITE
LIMITATION

NEED TO HAVE WEBPAGE OPEN ALL THE TIME TO SEE IF THERE ARE MISSING CHILDREN

UNABLE TO TRACK EXACT LOCATION OF CHILD

IMPROVEMENT

LED LIGHT CAN BE USED TO ALERT FACILITATORS IF CHILD GOES MISSING
RASPBERRY PI AT DIFFERENT ZONES TO TRACK EXACT LOCATION
RASPBERRY PI WITH LED LIGHT TO BE PLACED AT SHOPS TO ASK FOR HELP IF A CHILD GOES MISSING
